About XOOLO MARKET

XOOLO MARKET is a Somali-run platform dedicated to connecting livestock sellers and buyers across Somalia. Our goal is to support local herders, promote trust in trade, and celebrate the traditional livestock culture of our country.

We are committed to offering only the healthiest camels, cows, goats, sheep, and chickens, raised in the natural beauty of Somali pastures. Whether you are a buyer or a seller, we strive to make the process easy, safe, and culturally rooted.

Join us in strengthening the future of Somali livestock trade.
