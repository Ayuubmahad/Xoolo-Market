About XOOLO MARKET

XOOLO MARKET is a 100% Somali-owned digital marketplace created to revolutionize the way livestock is bought and sold across Somalia. We are proud to support our nomads, herders, and small-scale farmers by giving them access to modern tools and wider markets.

🌿 Our Mission

To empower Somali livestock sellers by connecting them with buyers across the country and beyond, using trusted, transparent, and technology-driven solutions.

🐪 What We Offer

  • Verified listings of healthy livestock: camels, cows, goats, sheep, and chickens
  • Safe and trusted seller profiles with photos and location info
  • Easy communication between buyer and seller — direct contact forms
  • Sell With Us program to help farmers post their animals for sale

🌍 Why Choose Us?

We know Somali culture, terrain, and people — because we are Somali. XOOLO MARKET is built with Somali values in mind: trust, community, and honesty in trade. Our system is designed to work well in both cities and remote areas, even with limited internet.

🕋 Trust & Ethics

We believe in halal trade that follows Islamic values. All listings must be truthful, and prices must be fair. As the Prophet ﷺ said:
